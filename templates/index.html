Understanding the impact and solutions for electronic waste management

What is E-Waste?

E-waste includes discarded devices like phones, laptops, batteries, and chargers. It contains hazardous materials like lead and mercury, posing risks if not handled properly.

Why It Matters

Improper disposal pollutes soil and water, contributing to 70% of global toxic waste. Recycling reduces harm and recovers valuable resources.

E-Waste Assistant is a pioneering initiative launched in 2025 to tackle India's growing e-waste crisis. Founded by a team of environmental enthusiasts and tech experts, we aim to reduce environmental pollution and promote a circular economy.
